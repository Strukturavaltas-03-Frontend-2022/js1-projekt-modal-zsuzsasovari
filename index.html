5 Interesting Pharmacy Facts

Agatha Christie was a pharmacy technician and used her experiences as inspiration for her mysteries. In 1886, pharmacist John S. Pemberton created Coca-Cola as a treatment for most common ailments. Paracetamol overdose causes depletion of glutathione, the compound responsible for ejecting toxic NAPQI. Opioids -such as fentanyl, morphine, heroin and oxycodone- stimulate the parasympathetic nervous system, causing constricted pinpoint pupils. There are three main drugs used in the treatment of alcoholism. These include disulfiram, acamprosate, and naltrexone.
